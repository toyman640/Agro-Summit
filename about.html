AGRICULTURAL ENTHUSIASTS GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

The Agricultural Enthusiast Summit is a gathering of like-minded individuals who share a passion for agriculture and its potential to shape the future. This summit brings together farmers, agronomists, researchers, policymakers, and industry experts to discuss innovative ideas, emerging trends, and best practices in the field of agriculture. This summit provides a platform for sharing ideas, networking, and promoting the sustainable development of agriculture. Participants can expect to engage in workshops, panel discussions, and keynote speeches from experts in the field. The Agricultural Enthusiast Summit is a must-attend event for anyone passionate about agriculture and its impact on society.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about AE Global Summit 2023!
aesummit2023@aenig.org

Agricultural Enthusiast Global Summit 2023 Logo

The logo of AE Global Summit 2023 was decided through the tech-agro competition from 14. February to 16. MarchThe winning designer, Adebola Adebayo, was thrilled to receive the prize money and recognition for their work. The logo was immediately implemented on the summit's website, packaging, and social media platforms, and it has been received positively by delegates.
